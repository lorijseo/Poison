Learn about chemical compatibility

When it comes to cleaning and disinfecting your home, it's easy to fall into the trap that using a variety of cleaning products will be a sure way of killing germs and leaving your home spotless. However, many cleaning prodcuts contain strong chemicals that when mixed can cause toxic mixture. Exposure to toxic mixture can result in mild to extreme irritation, burn, and damages to your body.

Peracetic Acid

Effect

Effects: A colorless liquid that can severly irritate lungs and burn skin and eyes.

Mixing Hydrogen Peroxide and Vinegar can create Peracetic Acid. You can use both of these ingredients to keep surfaces clean and disinfected as long as one completely dries before using the other.

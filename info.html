What are chemical cards?

This is a  Product card

 PRODUCT cards have  descriptions that may include: simple description of product or important notable ingredients

This is a  Nullify card

 NULLIFY cards have  descriptions that may include: the non-hazardous effects of the mixture or non-cleaning benefits

This is an  Effect card

 EFFECT cards have hazard  descriptions that may include: characteristics of hazard or symptoms of hazard exposure

 Ingredient cards have cleaning  advantages and  disadvantages

LIBRARY

 PRODUCT card

A  product card refers to a store bought cleaning product or brand

Every  product card will contain an informative  description that contains the best use of the product and some potentially hazardous ingredients.

 NULLIFY card

A  nullify card refers to an effect of mixing cleaning products that are non-hazardous and ineffective for cleaning

Every  nullify card will contain a generic  description that mentions that the mixture was not hazardous.

 EFFECT card

An  effect card refers to a potential dangerous hazard caused by mixing cleaning ingredients or products

Every  effect card will contain a warning  description that highlights the identification and symptoms of exposure.

 INGREDIENT card

An  ingredient card refers to a common cleaning ingredient or a general product used for cleaning.

Every  ingredient card will contain  advantages and  disadvantages for cleaning.

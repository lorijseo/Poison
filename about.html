The Story

This project idea started with a phone call.

My friend had just finished cleaning her bathroom when her eyes started burning. She felt extremely dizzy and nauseous. Her symptoms hit suddenly, and we didn't know why.

She mixed her cleaning products.

Until this health scare, I never pieced together the abrasiveness of these products and how harmful and dangerous these are to our health.

This was the beginning of this [NAME]. It allows us to learn about chemical compatibility and keep track of the chemicals in our cleaning supplies.
